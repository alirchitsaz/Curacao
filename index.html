Curaçao sits outside the Atlantic hurricane belt. June is warm and reliable — average highs ~89°F (32°C), water ~80°F (27°C), 6–7 light-shower days in the month, and constant easterly trade winds (~16 kt). Showers tend to be short bursts in the early morning or late at night. UV index is consistently very high — sunscreen is non-negotiable.
