Trip overview.

A working reference for nine days at Mambo Beach. Pricing and operating data verified through searches dated October 2025–April 2026 (sources cited on each card). Confirm prices and exact times with operators within 1–2 weeks of arrival.

June in Curaçao: highs ~89°F, water ~80°F, ~6 light-shower days in the month, easterly trade winds 14–18 kt, UV 10–11. Curaçao sits outside the Atlantic hurricane belt — one of the most weather-reliable Caribbean destinations. Showers tend to be short bursts, often early morning or after dark.
